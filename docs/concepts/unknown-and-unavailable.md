# Unknown and Unavailable

Unknown means a value or conclusion is not established. Unavailable means a
specific explanation cannot be produced from the supplied inputs. Neither is an
error to conceal or a blank to fill with a guess.

The repository prefers an explicit absence, limitation, or unavailable
explanation over fabricated certainty. Callers must not translate these states
into zero confidence, negative evidence, diagnosis, or recommendation without a
separate governed model.
