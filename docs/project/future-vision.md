# Future Vision

The long-term vision is infrastructure capable of supporting versioned,
source-governed, multilingual agricultural knowledge with explicit provenance and
explanations. Potential future consumers may include research, curation, and
decision-support systems.

None of those consumers, knowledge corpora, graph stores, diagnostic models,
recommendation policies, regulatory services, or production deployments is
implemented by the current Runtime. Future knowledge authoring must follow the
Knowledge Track; future product intelligence must receive its own scientific,
ethical, legal, security, and architecture governance.
