# Frequently Asked Questions

## Why no AI or LLM?

The current scope establishes explicit evidence, identity, contracts, and
determinism. Generative behavior would require separate governance and must not
hide unsupported transformation.

## Why no diagnosis, recommendation, ranking, or confidence score?

Those are distinct scientific and product decisions beyond read-only knowledge
infrastructure. Explanation does not authorize them.

## Why official sources and deterministic outputs?

Source authority and repeatable results make review, correction, and change
impact traceable. They do not independently guarantee correctness.

## Why a read-only Runtime and explicit composition?

Callers retain ownership of data and control. No hidden registry, loader, network,
database, or default knowledge state appears during import or service creation.

## Why synthetic examples? Is real agricultural knowledge bundled?

Synthetic records demonstrate engineering contracts without making unreviewed
agricultural claims. The installed distribution bundles no usable knowledge base.

## Can this be used as a production service today?

No production-readiness claim is made. The HTTP package is an injected FastAPI
app factory, not a server or deployment security stack.

## Can a consumer build diagnosis on top?

Only after separately governing observation, scientific evidence, regulation,
safety, uncertainty, oversight, product responsibility, and suitable APIs. Current
contracts do not certify such a product.

## How do the Engineering and Knowledge Tracks differ?

Engineering governs code and contracts. Knowledge governance controls sources,
terminology, claims, scientific review, and promotion. Neither silently approves
the other. See [governance boundaries](../maintainers/governance-boundaries.md).
