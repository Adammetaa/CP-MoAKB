# Maintainer Review Checklist

- Scope and Design Freeze are explicit.
- Implementation, docs, tests, ADRs, and RAS documents agree.
- Public/API/schema/package version impact is classified.
- Input, ordering, errors, composition, and dependency direction remain explicit.
- Synthetic-data and official-source policies are respected.
- No hidden I/O, network, environment, persistence, plugin, or mutable global state.
- Security, documentation, examples, release, and artifact verifiers pass as relevant.
- Limitations are stated without production, certification, or efficacy claims.
- Generated outputs and caches are removed.
- Publishing, tagging, release creation, and credential use have separate approval.
