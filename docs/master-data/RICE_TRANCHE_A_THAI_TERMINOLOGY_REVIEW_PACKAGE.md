# Rice Tranche A Thai Terminology Review Package

- Status: **Proposals for specialist review only; no Thai label is approved**
- Prepared: 2026-07-14
- Framework rule: BBCH codes are language-neutral and are never translated
- Scientific-name rule: *Oryza sativa* L. remains unchanged and is never a Thai translation

This package gives a qualified Thai Language or Terminology Reviewer and the applicable domain reviewer the smallest term-by-term decision set. `Source-backed` means only that the displayed Thai form occurs in the cited approved source; it does not prove exact equivalence to the English authority concept. `Editorial translation` and `Transliteration` are editor proposals, not sourced facts. `Unresolved` means no defensible preferred form is proposed.

Every final-decision cell is intentionally blank. The reviewer must enter `Approve`, `Approve with Correction`, `Reject`, or `Defer`, plus identity, date, and rationale on the applicable review sheet.

## Unresolved review points

- No qualified named Thai reviewer or translator/editor is authorized.
- No approved official Thai BBCH source has been identified; all eight stage forms are editorial proposals.
- Exact Thai source support is absent for root, leaf lamina, and spikelet, and remains inadequate for exact caryopsis identity.
- Source-backed `ลำต้น`, `ใบ`, `กาบใบ`, `รวง`, and `เมล็ดข้าว` still require concept-mapping review.
- `ระยะตั้งท้อง`, `การออกรวง`, `ช่อดอกย่อย`, and `ผลแบบธัญพืช` carry material scope or usage ambiguity.

Until authorized decisions are recorded, future candidate Thai preferred-label positions remain explicit `unknown` or `provisional` as the validation profile permits; this package does not authorize authoring.
