## Rights and use decision

Permitted use is limited to source metadata, external identifiers, concise normalized labels where explicitly approved, and original paraphrases of structured facts with attribution and exact locators. Public availability does not imply public-domain status. The pilot must not copy long passages, complete tables, images, diagrams, layouts, or substantial publication content.

Where rights remain unresolved, only bibliographic metadata, a stable link, and original assessment notes may be retained. Any broader extraction requires a Source and Rights Reviewer decision recorded against the exact claim and method.
