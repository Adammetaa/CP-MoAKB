# Rice Tranche A Review Assignment Decision Matrix

- Status: Required assignments defined; all named assignments remain unfilled
- Prepared: 2026-07-14
- Applies to: prerequisite decisions and the future 18-entity structural Tranche A

`Author` means the person who prepares the proposal or future candidate content. Every decision must preserve the reviewer identity, date, scope, evidence, outcome, rationale, conflicts, and corrections. Permitted outcomes are only `Approve`, `Approve with Correction`, `Reject`, or `Defer` unless a row explicitly governs release.

## Separation-of-duty rule

No individual may be the author, sole specialist reviewer, sole Evidence Reviewer, and final release approver for the same record set. Combining any two functions requires documented competence, consent, conflict review, and an independent decision wherever the matrix names a secondary or final role. Combining functions never allows a missing specialty to be inferred.
