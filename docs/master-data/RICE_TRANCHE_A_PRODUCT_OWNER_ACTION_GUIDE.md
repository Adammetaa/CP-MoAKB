# Rice Tranche A Product Owner Action Guide

- Status: Action guide only; Sprint-016 Tranche A remains blocked
- Current gate: **FAIL — DO NOT START CANDIDATE AUTHORING**

The Product Owner closes staffing and decision-record gaps; the role does not replace specialist review. No candidate directory, YAML, identifier allocation, production data, relationship, validator, or schema may be created while the entry checklist fails.

## Required actions

1. **Nominate or authorize named reviewers.** Complete every mandatory function in the [Reviewer Nomination Register](RICE_TRANCHE_A_REVIEWER_NOMINATION_REGISTER.md). Use real identities only with the nominee's knowledge.
2. **Provide qualification evidence or role basis.** Link concise, verifiable evidence of competence or a documented organizational appointment. Do not infer expertise from a name, employer, job title alone, or prior participation.
3. **Confirm consent.** Record that each nominee accepts the exact review scope and repository attribution. No silence-as-consent.
4. **Declare conflicts.** Obtain and record a conflict-of-interest declaration, including `none declared` where applicable, plus any mitigation.
5. **Confirm separation of duties.** Apply the [Review Assignment Matrix](RICE_TRANCHE_A_REVIEW_ASSIGNMENT_MATRIX.md). No person may be author, sole specialist reviewer, sole Evidence Reviewer, and final release approver for the same set.
6. **Obtain review decisions.** Have authorized reviewers complete the [Crop-Scope Review Sheet](RICE_TRANCHE_A_CROP_SCOPE_REVIEW_SHEET.md), [Plant-Structure Review Sheet](RICE_TRANCHE_A_PLANT_STRUCTURE_REVIEW_SHEET.md), and [BBCH Terminology Review Sheet](RICE_TRANCHE_A_BBCH_TERMINOLOGY_REVIEW_SHEET.md), using the [Thai Terminology Package](RICE_TRANCHE_A_THAI_TERMINOLOGY_REVIEW_PACKAGE.md). Corrections must remain explicit.
7. **Return signed or repository-recorded decisions.** Each decision needs reviewer identity, authorization reference, scope, outcome, date, rationale, conflicts, and correction disposition. A pull request review or committed decision record is acceptable if it contains the same audit fields and consented attribution.
8. **Request Chief Architect gate review.** After every prerequisite is resolved, request a review of the completed package, checklist, Design Freeze, format constraints, validation plan, and separation of duties. Only a subsequent explicit start authorization may permit candidate authoring.

## Product Owner role boundary

The Product Owner may serve as:

- Product Owner; and
- possibly Master Data Curator or operational coordinator when explicitly authorized and when independent review is preserved.

The Product Owner must not be represented as a qualified taxonomy, botany, evidence, source/rights, or Thai language/terminology specialist without a documented competence or organizational-authority basis that is reviewed for the exact scope. Product priority cannot waive evidence, rights, specialist, architecture, or conflict requirements.

## Package return check

Before requesting Chief Architect review, confirm:

- every mandatory nomination is `Authorized`, with consent and conflicts recorded;
- every review-sheet decision is complete and attributable;
- required corrections are implemented in governance documents without creating candidate data;
- all material specialist disagreements are resolved or explicitly deferred;
- the entry checklist has been independently re-evaluated row by row;
- no actual candidate ID, candidate directory, record, production data, or frozen-component change exists; and
- the requested action is a gate decision, not silent authorization inferred from completed paperwork.

Forms alone do not close a gate. Until authorized people complete the decisions and the Chief Architect records an explicit start decision, the controlling result remains **FAIL — DO NOT START CANDIDATE AUTHORING**.
