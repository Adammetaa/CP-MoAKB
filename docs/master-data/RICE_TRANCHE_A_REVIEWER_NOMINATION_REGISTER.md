# Rice Tranche A Reviewer Nomination Register

- Status: Empty nomination register; no person is nominated or authorized
- Prepared: 2026-07-14
- Gate effect: none; Sprint-016 Tranche A remains blocked

This register is the controlled place for the Product Owner to record reviewer nominations. Blank fields mean **not provided**, never implied approval. A nomination becomes `Authorized` only after identity, qualification or role basis, consent, conflict declaration, permitted scope, and separation of duty are recorded and accepted by the Product Owner or the stated escalation authority.

Allowed nomination statuses are: `Not Nominated`, `Nominated`, `Qualification Under Review`, `Authorized`, `Declined`, and `External Review Required`. No other status may be substituted.

## Completion procedure

1. The Product Owner enters a real nominee only with the person's knowledge.
2. Record a stable qualification-evidence reference or an explicit organizational role basis; do not paste unnecessary personal data.
3. Record affirmative consent, a conflict declaration, permitted scope, and any limitations.
4. Check the [Review Assignment Matrix](RICE_TRANCHE_A_REVIEW_ASSIGNMENT_MATRIX.md) for prohibited role combinations.
5. Set `Authorized` only through a repository-recorded Product Owner decision or an applicable documented authority.
6. Retain declined or superseded nominations in history; do not silently overwrite them.

Completing this register does not itself approve a term, mapping, scope decision, candidate record, or Sprint-016 start.
