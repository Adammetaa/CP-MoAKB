# Knowledge Governance Standards

Knowledge Governance Standards (KGS) govern the people, roles, authority,
review, publication, conflict management, accountability, and auditability of
CP-MoAKB knowledge work.

KGS MUST conform to the [Knowledge Constitution](../constitution/knowledge-constitution.md).
KGS governs who may act and decide; [Knowledge Authoring Standards](../README.md)
govern what authored knowledge and its evidence must satisfy. KGS MUST NOT
modify Runtime, APIs, schemas, parsers, validation, registries, engineering
contracts, or the authority of an accepted ADR or active RAS.

| [KGS-005](KGS-005-publication-governance.md) | Active | 1.0 | Knowledge releases, freezes, emergency publication, retraction, and withdrawal |
| [KGS-006](KGS-006-audit-and-transparency.md) | Active | 1.0 | Review, decision, evidence, publication, revision, and audit records |

Repository presence, review completion, acceptance, and publication are distinct.
No KGS document creates agricultural knowledge, approves master data, or
authorizes a public repository push, tag, release, artifact upload, or package
publication.
